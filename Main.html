About Us

AeroMIT was founded by 4 mechanical engineers from MIT, Manipal in 2009. We are a team of over 20 interdisciplinary undergraduate students of Manipal University who aim to conceptualize, design, fabricate, manufacture, test and fly a complex, stable and portable model aircraft with minimal empty weight, maximum payload carrying capacity and high endurance. AeroMIT participates in SAE AeroDesign event hosted by SAE International and Lockheed Martin which is held in Lakeland, Florida, Van Nuys, California and Forth Worth, Texas, USA.

Apart from this, We also take part in various competitions across India held at different colleges such as IIT Bombay, NIT Surathkal, NIT Calicut.

The mission of the competition is to train the future engineers in handling all the aspects of a project including the raising of funds, maintainance of a budget and building an aircraft feasible for remote and rapid deployments. This project provides us with technical knowledge beyond the scope of text books along with exposure to the professional world and improving our managerial skills.

We also undertake the design and fabrication of various multi-rotors and drones to fulfill different mission requirements.

This is followed by extensive research, analysis and assimilation of information.
